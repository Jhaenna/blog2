The Ideal Summer Capsule Outfit


In this blog post, Jheanna share the items you need in your closet to create summer looks for almost all occasions


The Ideal Summer Capsule Outfit The most awaited season of the year is summer, but it is also the shortest and least predictable (especially if you live in Canada). The majority of the time, we don't know what to dress because it can be hot or cold at any one time. Because of this, we put up a summer capsule outfit using the goods we always have on hand. Each one makes getting dressed more simpler and is ideal for mixing and matching.


The brands that Olivia and Laura regularly wear, such as Zara, H&M, Aerie, and Dynamite, are used in every ensemble. Want to buy our looks? For your buying convenience, every item will be connected!

Capsule 1: Walking around a park

Our summer wardrobe must-haves include this ensemble. A laid-back appearance that could be dressed up for a girls' day out or toned down for a short park stroll.

Capsule 2: Performing Tasks

With these cozy biker shorts, channel your inner Princess Diana! Running errands in a biker shorts and an oversized graphic t-shirt or sweatshirt is the definition of easy cool.

Capsule 3: Sunday Barbecue

A summer dress of any length is a requirement for any summer outfit! It is the simplest costume to put together for an elegantly casual style, making it ideal for the Sunday BBQ with your family!
